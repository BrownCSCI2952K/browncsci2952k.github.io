Course Structure

This initial structure is subject to change.

The course structure involves reading papers, writing paper reviews, presenting a research paper (with a group or individually, TBD), leading and participating in discussions, and doing a group research project. The course will start with a few introductory lectures on paper reading techniques, tips on writing paper reviews, topics and relevant papers on 3D vision and ML, and project ideas. These lectures will provide the background to do well in the rest of the course.

Each subsequent week will involve reading two research papers and writing a review for each paper. One or more students will be responsible for presenting each paper and everyone will participate in classroom discussion of these papers. In parallel, we will form groups and brainstorm ideas for the group research project. There will be mid-term and final presentations/reports for this project.

There is no final exam. Please make sure to familiarize yourself with this course's policies.

Capstone credit or auditing.
