Course Structure

This initial structure is subject to change.

The course structure involves reading papers, presenting a research paper (with a group), leading and participating in discussions, and doing a group research project.

Each subsequent week will involve reading research papers on a related topic. A group of students will present each topic and a review committee will participate in classroom discussion of these papers. In parallel, we will form groups and brainstorm ideas for the group research project. There will be mid-term and final check-ins for this project.


There is no final exam. Please make sure to familiarize yourself with this course's policies.
